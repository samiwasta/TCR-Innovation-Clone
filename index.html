Campus Ambassador

Campus Ambassadors are responsible for Promotion of the Internship Programs along with all the other Company Promotions. Sharing the Promotional messages on various Social Media Platforms like WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, etc. is one of the main responsibilities.

HR Manager

Interns are responsible for Hiring a team of individuals and Mentoring them. Leading their team and making sure they are carrying out their tasks is one of the major responsibilities.

Sales & Marketing

responsible for researching and developing marketing opportunities and planning and implementing new sales plans. The sales and marketing manager will also manage both the marketing and the sales staff and will perform managerial duties to meet the company's operations goals.

Technical Blogger

Interns are responsible for publishing Blogs, Research Papers, Technical Papers or any kind of textual content on behalf of TCR Innovation on the given area or subject. Individuals who are avid about writing and have writing skills or are looking for the opportunity to improve their skills ideal for this Internship.

Content Creator & Editor

producing marketing copy to advertise our products, writing blog posts about industry-related topics and promoting our content on social media. To be successful in this role, you should have experience with digital publishing and generating traffic and leads for new business.

Digital Marketing

Interns are responsible for promoting the Social Media front of TCR Innovation while majorly Researching and Experimenting with ways to improve it. Publishing Content on the Social Media handles while bringing in Paid Enrollments and handling Marketing is one of the major parts of this Internship.
